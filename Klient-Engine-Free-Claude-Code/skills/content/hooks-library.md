## Quick Reference — Hook Categories
1. Educational Hooks — teaching, tips, tutorials, how-tos
2. Comparison Hooks — before/after, vs, contrasts
3. Myth Busting Hooks — "everyone thinks X but actually..."
4. Storytelling Hooks — personal stories, journeys
5. Authority Hooks — credibility, results, proof
6. Day in the Life Hooks — behind the scenes, routines
7. Random / Engagement Hooks — pattern interrupts, curiosity gaps

## Top 10 Universal Hook Starters (for quick use)
1. "I made $[X] doing [thing] and nobody talks about this..."
2. "Stop [common action] — here's what actually works."
3. "This one thing changed how I [result] forever."
4. "If you're a [audience], you need to hear this."
5. "Nobody told me [thing] when I started [niche]."
6. "I spent [time/money] so you don't have to."
7. "The [industry] doesn't want you to know this."
8. "Here's the [number]-second version of [complex thing]."
9. "POV: You finally figured out how to [dream result]."
10. "This is what [result] actually looks like in [time frame]."

## Usage Rules for Video Hooks
- Must be under 3 seconds when spoken aloud
- Write as spoken dialogue — no hashtags, no emojis, no caption language
- Mix styles across a batch: curiosity, bold claim, pattern interrupt, story, controversy
- First word matters most — start with action, number, or "you/your"
