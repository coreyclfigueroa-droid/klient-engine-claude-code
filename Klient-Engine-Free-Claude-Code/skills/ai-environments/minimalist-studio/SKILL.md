## Visual Description
A clean, near-white studio environment with a very subtle warm-gray gradient background. No hard features — just soft, even light that wraps the subject cleanly. The background is not pure white — it has a slight paper texture and the faintest shadow gradient falling from the top. This is the neutral backdrop that makes the avatar the only thing in frame.
